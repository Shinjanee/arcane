Fabulous is a classic logotype script. 
Bold but delicate, this script has both class and genuinity. 
The typeface also includes a white, outlined version called Fabulous Outline. 

Write _ after a word to get a swash. 
Example: Fabulous_

With ligatures activated, write _ and a number for more swashes. 
Example: Lovely_5 Font_2
